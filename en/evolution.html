Timeline

Timeline of Advertising Evolution (1850 - 2085).

• 1850-1900: The Beginnings of Advertising

• 1880: Advertising in newspapers
The emergence of printed advertisements in newspapers and magazines marks a turning point.

• 1900-1950: The Golden Age of Advertising

• 1920: Radio advertising
The advent of radio transforms advertising into an auditory format, with companies sponsoring programs.

• 1950-2000: The Era of Creativity

• 1990: Internet advertising
The rise of the World Wide Web introduces online advertising, with the first banner ads revolutionizing the landscape.

2020-2085: The Future of Advertising

• 2035: Holographic advertising
The first holographic advertisements emerge, projecting interactive 3D images in public spaces, engaging passersby.

• 2045

• 2045: Artificial intelligence and personalization
Advertising becomes hyper-personalized, adjusting content in real-time based on consumer preferences.

• 2085

• 2085: Integrated augmented reality advertising
Holographic ads emerge from screens and interact directly with consumers, inspired by Minority Report. Users can engage with holographic product representations, try on clothing virtually, or receive real-time personalized service recommendations.
